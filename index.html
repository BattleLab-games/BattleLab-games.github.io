About the game

BuildNow GG is a fun online 3rd person survival shooter in which you can build and shoot!

Fight other players around the world, Get a Victory Royale Win and become the World's Best Fighter. Construct forts and edit buildings to hide from fire or catch enemy in a trap. You can play with random players or invite your friends to the lobby

There are 4 modes available in the game:

  • FreeBuild - A large island where you can freely play PVP and practice without any time limits
  • V-Arena - 1v1 Arena, the Last man standing wins!
  • BoxFight - 1v1 BoxFight, the Last man standing wins!
  • Aim Training - Train to improve your shooting and building skills!
